Who should come?

Anyone who works with data, whether you’ve just started school or a battle-scarred veteran with a thousand-yard stare.

Who should speak?

Anyone who’s got some beef about something data related, we don’t bother checking credentials.

Do I need a webcam to speak?

No, but it’s sorta more fun if you do.

What’s the format?

Very informal. We go around to introduce ourselves at the beginning. Then ranters are given 5 minutes to talk about their chosen data topic. When then comment, discuss, ask questions for another 5+ minutes. If a conversation develops that's intersting, we keep going. Moderators can pull us back in order if the discussion goes too far off track.

Can I just watch?

Yes, but it’d be more fun to discuss or at least use chat. If someone is restreaming to another service, you may choose to watch that option too.

Will it be recorded?

Yes. However, the sessions are way too long. The organizer is exploring ways to cut and edit the video into a highlight reel. But there is no guarantee that anything said or shown on screen will be included/excluded from the video. As a general rule, don't say or show anything that you don't want any random person on the internet to know. Someone may be making their own private full recordings.

Do I have to actually drink alcohol?

No. We’re all just looking at screens and talking into a computer. Drink whatever you want, or nothing at all. It’s just totally acceptable to be drinking alcohol if you so choose.
